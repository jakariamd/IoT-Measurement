Mandrill Login What is Mandrill? Mandrill is a paid Mailchimp add-on, and allows clients to send one-to-one transactional emails triggered by user actions, like requesting a password or placing an order. They're powerful touchpoints between you and your customers, so we've made it easier to make the most of them.
