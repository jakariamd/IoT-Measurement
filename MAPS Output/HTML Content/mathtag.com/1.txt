</span><span>  Search</span><ul>  <li>May we suggest...</li></ul><span>  Search</span><ul>  <li>Process For Consumers</li></ul></div> <div><h1>  For Consumers: BBB National Programs Dispute Resolution Process</h1><p>  Welcome to the BBB National Programs consumer complaint system. BBB National Programs, a non-profit organization based in the United States, helps individual consumers resolve privacy complaints under the  Data Privacy Framework Program.  </p><p>  If you are an individual in the  European Union, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, or other participating countries with a privacy complaint against a participating U.S. business, you may submit the complaint to our program. Our process is free of charge for individual consumers.  </p></div> <div><span>  File a Complaint</span><p>  We will review all eligible complaints and help you and the participating business reach a resolution as described in our program’s <a href="/programs/all-programs/dpf/ProcedureRules" target="_blank"> Rules </a>. A Participating Business must comply with BBB National Programs' final determination of any dispute. However, our process is non-binding on the consumer, which means that using our dispute resolution
        process will not affect your legal rights as an individual. </p></div> <div><h2>  <strong> Is your complaint eligible for resolution? </strong></h2><p>  Your complaint must concern personal data about you that was collected in a covered country and received in the United States by a Participating Business.</p><p>  <strong> 1. Eligible individual. </strong>
</p><p>  Your complaint concerns personal data about you (or your child under 13 years old).</p><p>  <strong> 2. Covered country. </strong></p><p>  Your personal data was collected in one of the following countries:</p></div> <div><td>  <strong> European Union  </strong></td><td>  Austria </td><p>  Belgium </p><p>  Bulgaria </p><p>  Croatia </p><p>  Cyprus </p><p>  Republic </p><p>  Denmark </p><p>  Estonia </p><td>  Finland </td><p>  France </p><p>  Germany </p><p>  Greece </p><p>  Hungary </p><p>  Ireland </p><p>  Italy </p><p>  Latvia </p><td>  Lithuania </td><p>  Luxembourg </p><p>  Malta </p><p>  Netherlands </p><p>  Poland </p><p>  Portugal </p><p>  Romania </p><p>  Slovakia </p><td>  Slovenia </td><p>  Spain </p><p>  Sweden </p></div> <div><td>  <strong> European Economic Area  </strong></td><td>  Iceland </td><p>  Liechtenstein </p><p>  Norway </p><td>  <strong> The United Kingdom  </strong></td><td>  <strong> Switzerland </strong></td><p>  <strong> 3.     </p><p>  b. A Participating Business with BBB National Programs (you may search our list of current participants when you file your complaint).</p></div> <p><p>  <strong> 4.
