As the number of ways to enjoy TV shows, movies, live sports and news programs around the world continues to increase, it is more important than ever to promote TV and video content in engaging and informative ways to viewers. Access to local TV schedules and descriptive data for movies and shows on a global scale is critical for today’s entertainment providers to power their platforms and expand their international footprints.</p></div> <p><p>  Gracenote Global Video Data is the definitive collection of aggregated, normalized international video data, imagery and IDs. That’s why top entertainment brands and platforms around the world rely on Gracenote to power their guide, search and discovery experiences, empowering audiences to find and enjoy their favorite shows.</p></p> <p><p>  Gracenote Global Video Data includes aggregate, normalized information and imagery for millions of programs across linear and streaming around the globe. Gracenote’s editorial teams adhere to rigorous guidelines to ensure the highest standards in data quality and reliability worldwide. A standard data format and Gracenote IDs (TMS) enable connections between TV schedule and on-demand content, turbo-charging content search and discovery experiences for viewers.</p></p> <div><p>
