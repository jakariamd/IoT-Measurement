Preference cookies enable a website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, like your preferred language or the region that you are in.</li></ul></div> <ul><ul>  <li><strong> Statistic cookies  </strong>are used by analytical services to track and analyze users’ engagements with online sources in order to improve their services.
