How do we collect data?</h2><p>  Smart uses technologies like cookies, tags, and mobile SDKs to collect the data and store it on a User’s device.</p><p>  Cookies are small text files that contain a string of characters, stored in the User’s device. They allow us to recognize and remember the user’s browser, preferences and other information.</p><p>  The Smart tag is a small piece of code that is inserted into a Client’s website source code, which allows them to have access and manage our services.</p></div> <p><p>  An SDK, or Software Development Kit, is the software used by our Clients to integrate Smart to their mobile application (iOS and Android).</p></p> <p><p>  Web beacons are used to help our Clients understand how visitors interact with their websites. Pixel tags are transparent images, iFrames, or JavaScript that are placed on a site.
