</li></ul><span>  Your Ads</span></div> <div><h1>  Analytics and Reporting. </h1><p>  <b> Time to read: </b> 2 minutes</p></div> <div><h2>   Analytics and Reporting. </h2></div> <div><h2>  At a glance</h2><p>  <b> We use personal data to conduct Analytics and Reporting </b> for our ad program, which helps us improve our products and services.</p><p>  <b> There’s no information that directly identifies you </b> in the reports we share with other companies.</p><p>  <b> You can choose to opt out— </b>just tell us not to use your personal data for Analytics and Reporting.</p></div> <div><h2>  Why we analyze your data</h2><p>  We’re looking for patterns to help us understand consumer interests, behaviors, and trends.</p><p>  After analyzing the data, we create reports for our use and for sharing with other companies. This is often called Analytics and Reporting.  </p><p>  For example, we might create a report showing how often a game app was downloaded in the past year across a large number of users.  </p><p>  We aggregate your personal data that doesn’t directly identify you with the information available from thousands of other consumers. For example, we might compile a report that shows how many views an ad received and what percentage of viewers clicked on that ad. </p></div> <p><p>  We also take comprehensive steps to prevent these shared reports and analytics from containing data that directly identifies you. </p></p> <div><h2>  Data we collect for Analytics and Reporting</h2></div> <div><h3>  Phone data </h3><ul>  <li>Your network diagnostic information (Android phones only).</li><li>Your app usage data—including how long an app is open, how often you open an app, and whether an app is installed or uninstalled.</li><li>Your zip code.</li></ul></div> <div><h3>  Self-reported demographics data </h3><p>  While using certain products and services, you may provide information about yourself to us such as your age range—25-34, for example—and your gender identity. </p></div> <div><h2>  How to opt out</h2><ul>  <li>Go to our <b> Privacy Dashboard </b>.</li><li>Review the description under “Analytics and Reporting.”</li><li>If you want us to stop analyzing your personal data and using it to create reports, set the toggle to “OFF” or gray.</li></ul></div> <div><h2>  When we won’t use your data for Analytics and Reporting</h2><ul>  <li>You’ve designated your line as a Kids’ Line or if we know the information belongs to a child.</li><li>You use a company-liable T‑Mobile for Business or T‑Mobile for Government device.</li><li>You’re a T‑Mobile Prepaid, Assurance Wireless, or Metro lifeline customer.</li></ul><p>  For more information on Analytics and Reporting, read our <b>  Privacy Notice </b>.</p></div> <div><h2>  Related links</h2><ul>  <li>
